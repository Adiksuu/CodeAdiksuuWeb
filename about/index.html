About Me

Hi! I'm Kacper, on the web he says "Adiksuu" or "CodeAdiksuu" - Depending on the situation.

At the beginning of my learning I encountered many tools for creating pages, such as "WIX" or other similar tools, but I was not very interested in it, when I went deeper into the topic of creating pages,

I found many channels on YouTube presenting website development with HTML / CSS and at a later stage also JAVASCRIPT. Since then I have created a lot of all kinds of projects, most of them were training projects. At the age of 13, I decided to check if my projects were suitable for publication on the web, if they were noteworthy, after sending my main projects to all forums, many people were interested in my work, and then I started getting a lot of questions if I could create a website for a little money, since then I create websites on request at an affordable price.
